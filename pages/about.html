How it works

How do I download the app?

To download the Scoot app, you can search “Scoot” in both the App and Google Play stores. An even simpler way to do it would be to click the relevant link at the bottom of this page and you'll be re-directed to the correct page.

Can I find a nearby Scoots?

Yes, you can easily find nearby Scoots using the Scoot app. The app will show you all available Scoots in your vicinity on a map, making it simple to locate and rent one.

Do I need a license to ride??

In most locations, you do not need a license to ride a Scoot. However, we recommend checking your local regulations to be sure.

Safe driving

Should I wear a helmet?

Yes, we recommend that all riders wear a helmet when riding a Scoot. Helmets are available at most of our locations for a small fee.

How about the rules & regulations?

We recommend that all riders familiarize themselves with local laws and regulations before riding a Scoot. We also recommend taking our free online driving course to learn how to safely ride a Scoot.

What if I damage my scoot

If you damage a Scoot, please contact our customer service team as soon as possible. You can reach them via the app or by emailing at customer@scoot.com
